Free & Public

Bioregional Wiki

If you were to move to Odemira, what's all the information you'd need to become a good neighbor quickly? That's the wiki. Short, scannable articles about everything from soil types to local festivals — with a chat interface so you can just ask questions.

  • The Land — geology, boundaries, elevation
  • The Water — rivers, springs, water quality
  • The Weather — climate, fire risk, growing seasons
  • What Lives Here — biodiversity, species lists
  • What Grows Here — agriculture, land use
  • Who's Here — communities, experts
  • The Story — history, culture, festivals
  • The Rules — zoning, planning, protected areas

Private & Paid

Landbook

A private data vault for your specific land parcel. Draw your boundary on the map and we'll generate a comprehensive report — soil type, elevation, fire risk, species likely present, zoning, seasonal guidance. Then add your own knowledge on top.

  • Draw your boundary — we calculate area & perimeter
  • Auto-generated report from EU open data
  • Soil, weather, elevation, biodiversity
  • Fire risk, drought risk, flood risk
  • Zoning and protected area overlays
  • Add your own data — challenges, goals
  • Optional title deed upload (secure)
  • Seasonal growing calendar
