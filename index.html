What we analyze

We pull from 15+ APIs and satellite sources to build a complete picture of your land — from bedrock to biodiversity.

Elevation & Geology

SRTM elevation data, slope analysis, and bedrock geology from Macrostrat and LNEG geological survey.

Soil Profile

Texture, pH, organic carbon, nitrogen, and WRB classification from SoilGrids at 250m resolution.

Water & Flood Risk

Rivers, streams, wells, and springs from OpenStreetMap. Flood hazard zones from JRC and GloFAS forecasts.

Weather & Climate

7-day forecast, 30-year climate averages, frost dates, and drought index from Open-Meteo and IPMA.

Biodiversity

Species counts and photos from iNaturalist, threatened species alerts, and GBIF occurrence records.

Fire & Protected Areas

EFFIS fire danger rating, NASA FIRMS active fire detections, and Natura 2000 conservation zones.
